Our Process

6-Stage Scientific Cleaning

Our advanced cleaning process ensures complete sanitization and purification of your water storage systems.

1

Dewatering

An electrical submersible pump is used to pump out water from the tank completely.

Stage 1 Equipment
2

Sludge Removal

Sludge, mud and dirty water residue from previous stages is removed using a pump and wet vacuum cleaner.

Stage 2 Equipment
3

High Pressure Jet Cleaning

Complete tank is thoroughly cleaned from ceiling, walls & floor, leaving no speck of dirt, algae, mosses, bacteria & calcination.

Stage 3 Equipment
4

Vacuum Cleaning Process

Sedimentation is removed by a vacuum pump making the tank dirt free and completely clean.

Stage 4 Equipment
5

Anti-Bacterial Spray

Walls, ceilings and floors are made totally sterile from any bacterial attack using specially developed antibacterial agents.

Stage 5 Equipment
6

UV Radiation

A specially developed Ultra Violet germicide lamp is used to kill remaining suspended bacteria, pathogens, virus, mould and algae.
